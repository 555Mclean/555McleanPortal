Who We Are

A Community
We Call Home

555 McLean Ave is a resident-owned cooperative where neighbors look out for one another. Everything we do is guided by our shared commitment to a safe, welcoming building.

🏢

Our Building

A resident-owned cooperative in Yonkers, NY. As shareholders, each resident has a real stake in our community and a voice in how our home is run.

👥

Board of Directors

Elected by shareholders, our volunteer Board oversees day-to-day operations, building finances, and community policy. Meetings are open to all residents.

📜

House Rules

Our house rules keep the building comfortable for everyone. Copies are available from the managing agent or by emailing the board directly.

Maintenance

Help

Frequently Asked
Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from shareholders and residents.

Monthly maintenance fees are due on the 1st of each month. Payments received after the 10th may be subject to late charges. Contact the managing agent with any billing questions.

Use the Maintenance Request link in the Resources section to email the managing agent. Please include your apartment number and a clear description of the issue. For building emergencies, use the emergency contact in the Contact section.

Scroll to the Waiting Lists section on this page and complete the form for parking or storage. The board will contact you when a spot becomes available. Current wait times vary — submit early.

Monthly board meetings are generally held on the second Tuesday of each month in the Community Room. Confirmed dates are posted in the Meetings section above. All shareholders are welcome and encouraged to attend.

All moves must be scheduled in advance with building management. Moves are permitted weekdays 9am–5pm only. A refundable move-in/move-out deposit is required. Contact the managing agent to coordinate and obtain the building's move policy.

Subletting is permitted subject to board approval. You must submit an application, and the subtenant is subject to the same house rules as shareholders. Contact the board for the current subletting policy and application process.

All shareholders in good standing are eligible to vote. Ballots and candidate information are distributed prior to the annual meeting. You may vote in person at the meeting or by proxy — contact the board for proxy voting forms.

Use the contact form at the bottom of this page or email the board directly. For non-urgent matters, allow up to 5 business days for a response. Building emergencies should be reported to the emergency contact number in the Contact section.
