Who We Are

About Our Cooperative

🏢 Our Building

555 McLean Ave is a resident-owned cooperative community committed to maintaining a safe, welcoming, and well-managed home for all shareholders.

📋 Board of Directors

Elected by shareholders, our Board oversees operations, finances, and building policy. Board members volunteer their time for the community.

📜 House Rules

All residents are expected to follow co-op house rules. Copies are available from the managing agent or by request from the board.

🔧 Maintenance

For repairs and maintenance requests, contact the managing agent directly. Emergency issues should be reported immediately.
