June 20, 2026 · Safety Notice Safety

Parking Lot Speed Limit — 5 MPH, Watch for Children

For the safety of all residents, please be advised that a strict speed limit of 5 miles per hour is in effect throughout the building's parking lots and driveways at all times. Children are frequently present in these areas, and drivers must remain alert, proceed slowly, and yield to pedestrians at all times. Operators of all vehicles are required to exercise the utmost caution when entering, exiting, and maneuvering within the lots. Failure to observe the posted speed limit endangers the community and may result in the issuance of a formal violation notice. The Board thanks all residents for their cooperation in keeping our community safe.

June 20, 2026 · Reminder Policy

Pet Waste Policy — Please Clean Up After Your Pet

All residents and their guests are hereby reminded that pet owners are required to clean up after their pets at all times, whether indoors or outdoors. This obligation applies to all common areas, including but not limited to hallways, elevators, lobbies, stairwells, walkways, and the grounds of the property. Failure to promptly remove and properly dispose of pet waste constitutes a violation of the building's rules and regulations, and may result in the issuance of a formal violation notice and the imposition of applicable penalties. The Board thanks all residents for their cooperation in maintaining a clean, safe, and sanitary environment for the entire community.

June 8, 2026 · Safety Notice Action Required

Damaged Tree Near Garage Entrance — Safety Alert

Residents, please be aware that a tree section near the dumpster area at the end of the driveway has sustained damage and is currently resting against neighboring branches and overhead Verizon lines. The Fire Department visited the site this evening and assessed the situation. Because the tree sits beyond our fence line as part of the aqueduct property, responsibility for removal falls outside our jurisdiction. We have already reached out to the county Department of Public Works and are awaiting their direction on next steps. In the meantime, we ask everyone to take extra care when pulling in or out of the garage and when passing through that area on foot. Your safety is our priority. We will keep you updated as the situation develops.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from shareholders and residents.

Monthly maintenance fees are due on the 1st of each month. Payments received after the 10th may be subject to late charges. Contact the managing agent with any billing questions.

Use the Maintenance Request form — available from the Quick Actions bar at the top of the page. It walks you through a few quick steps and prepares a complete email to the managing agent. For building emergencies, use the emergency contact in the Contact section.

Scroll to the Waiting Lists section on this page and complete the form for parking or storage. The board will contact you when a spot becomes available. Current wait times vary — submit early.

Parking spots are offered from the waiting list in the order requests are received, with one key rule: shareholders who don't yet have a spot are given first priority. A second spot is only offered to a shareholder once everyone waiting for their first spot has been accommodated. A maximum of 2 parking spots per shareholder is permitted. When you sign up, you can tell the board whether you'd prefer an indoor or outdoor spot — or take whichever becomes available first.

Monthly board meetings are generally held on the second Tuesday of each month in the Community Room. Confirmed dates are posted in the Meetings section above. All shareholders are welcome and encouraged to attend.

All moves must be scheduled in advance with building management. Moves are permitted weekdays 9am–5pm only. A refundable move-in/move-out deposit is required. Contact the managing agent to coordinate and obtain the building's move policy.

Subletting is permitted subject to board approval. You must submit an application, and the subtenant is subject to the same house rules as shareholders. Contact the board for the current subletting policy and application process.

All shareholders in good standing are eligible to vote. Ballots and candidate information are distributed prior to the annual meeting. You may vote in person at the meeting or by proxy — contact the board for proxy voting forms.

Use the contact details at the bottom of this page or email the board directly. For non-urgent matters, allow up to 5 business days for a response. Building emergencies should be reported to the emergency contact number in the Contact section.
