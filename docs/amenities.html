Building Amenities & Policies

Day-to-day information for living at 555 McLean Ave — a resident-owned cooperative in Yonkers, NY.

Items marked to be confirmed depend on the building's current setup — the board will finalize the exact hours, locations, and schedules before this page is treated as official.

🧺 Laundry

  • Laundry room location: to be confirmed.
  • Hours of operation: to be confirmed — please be considerate of quiet hours when using machines.
  • Payment: to be confirmed (coin / card / app).
  • Please remove laundry promptly, clean lint traps, and report broken machines to the managing agent.

📦 Packages & Deliveries

  • Deliveries are left to be confirmed (lobby / package area). The building is not responsible for lost or stolen packages.
  • Retrieve packages promptly so common areas stay clear.
  • Large-item and furniture deliveries should be scheduled like a move — see the Move Policy.

♻️ Trash & Recycling

  • Trash and recycling area: to be confirmed.
  • Recycling is required by the City of Yonkers — separate paper/cardboard, metal/glass/plastic, and regular refuse.
  • Break down boxes. Do not leave trash in hallways, stairwells, or outside apartment doors.
  • Bulk items: arrange disposal per building procedure to be confirmed; do not leave them in common areas.

🔇 Quiet Hours & Guests

  • Quiet hours run 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM daily (see the House Rules for full details).
  • Residents are responsible for the conduct of their guests at all times.
  • Long-term guests and any subletting are subject to board approval.

🛠️ Superintendent & Management

  • Superintendent: name & hours to be confirmed.
  • Non-emergency repairs go to the managing agent via the Maintenance Request form.
  • For building emergencies, see Emergency Contacts.
