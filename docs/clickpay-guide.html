Paying Maintenance with ClickPay

ClickPay is the online portal for paying your monthly maintenance. Here's how to get set up.

You'll need your account / resident number to link your unit. If you don't have it, the managing agent (Gramatan Management) can provide it. Details marked to be confirmed depend on the building's ClickPay configuration.

1. Create your account

  1. Go to clickpay.com and choose Register.
  2. Enter your name, email, and a password to create your login.
  3. Verify your email address if prompted.

2. Connect your unit

  1. Search for the property — 555 McLean Ave (managed by Gramatan Management).
  2. Enter your account / resident number to link your apartment. Format to be confirmed by the managing agent.
  3. Confirm your unit details are correct.

3. Choose how to pay

  • Bank account (ACH/e-check): usually free — add your routing and account number.
  • Credit / debit card: accepted, but the processor may charge a convenience fee.

4. Set up autopay (recommended)

  • Enable AutoPay so your maintenance is paid automatically each month — this helps avoid late fees.
  • Maintenance is due on the 1st of each month; schedule autopay a few days ahead to be safe.
  • You can also make one-time payments anytime from your dashboard.

Need help?

  • ClickPay support: clickpay.com (Help / Live Chat).
  • Account questions: contact the managing agent, Gramatan Management, at (914) 654-1414.
