# 555 McLean Ave — House Rules (DRAFT for Board Adoption)

> **Status: DRAFT.** This is a template the Board can edit, approve, and then
> publish (as `house-rules.pdf` per `docs/README.md`). Every figure, time, and
> fee below is a common starting point — confirm or change each one to match
> the co-op's Proprietary Lease and Bylaws before adopting. Where the House
> Rules and the Proprietary Lease conflict, the Lease controls.

These House Rules apply to all shareholders, their household members, guests,
subtenants, and anyone acting on their behalf. They are adopted by the Board of
Directors under the authority of the Proprietary Lease and may be amended by the
Board at any time.

---

## 1. Noise & Quiet Enjoyment
- Quiet hours are **10:00 PM to 8:00 AM** daily, and all day on Sundays and
  legal holidays for construction/renovation noise.
- No resident shall make or permit noise that unreasonably disturbs neighbors at
  any hour (loud music, TVs, instruments, parties, barking, etc.).
- At least **80% of the floor area of each apartment** (excluding kitchens and
  bathrooms) must be covered with rugs or carpeting to reduce sound transmission.

## 2. Common Areas, Hallways & Lobbies
- Hallways, stairwells, lobbies, and landings must be kept clear at all times.
  Do not store shoes, mats, strollers, bicycles, furniture, or personal items in
  these areas — they are fire-egress paths.
- Do not place mats, signs, decorations, or shoe racks outside apartment doors.
- Bicycles, scooters, and carts may not be ridden in common areas and must be
  stored inside apartments or in designated storage only.
- Children may not play in hallways, stairwells, elevators, the lobby, or the
  parking areas.

## 3. Move-In / Move-Out
- All moves must be **scheduled in advance** with the managing agent.
- Permitted moving hours: **9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Monday–Saturday.** No moves on
  Sundays or legal holidays.
- A refundable **move deposit** (suggested **$500**) is required to cover any
  damage to common areas; the elevator must be padded for the move.
- See the separate **Move Policy** for full details.

## 4. Alterations & Renovations
- No structural, plumbing, electrical, flooring, or wall alterations may begin
  until a signed **Alteration Agreement** is approved by the Board.
- Contractors must provide proof of license and insurance naming the co-op as
  additional insured before work starts.
- Permitted construction hours: **9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Monday–Friday** (no
  weekends or holidays). No work that shuts off building water, gas, or
  electricity without prior written approval and notice to affected residents.

## 5. Pets
- Confirm the building's pet policy and reflect it here (e.g., dogs permitted /
  not permitted, weight or breed limits, registration, number of pets).
- All pets must be leashed in common areas and never left unattended there.
- Owners must **clean up after pets immediately**, indoors and out (see the
  building's Pet Waste Policy).
- No pet may create a nuisance through noise, odor, or aggression.

## 6. Garbage, Recycling & Sanitation
- Household trash must be bagged, tied, and placed in designated containers/chute
  only. Break down boxes and follow Yonkers recycling rules (paper/cardboard,
  metal/glass/plastic separated).
- No bulk items, furniture, mattresses, electronics, or construction debris in
  the regular trash — arrange bulk pickup through the managing agent.
- Do not leave trash in hallways, stairwells, or outside apartment doors.

## 7. Parking & Vehicles
- Parking spaces are assigned through the **parking waitlist**; spaces may not be
  sublet or transferred without Board approval.
- **5 MPH speed limit** in all lots and driveways; watch for children.
- No vehicle repairs, washing, or storage of inoperable/unregistered vehicles on
  the property. No blocking driveways, fire lanes, dumpsters, or other spaces.

## 8. Laundry Room
- Confirm hours and reflect them here (suggested **7:00 AM – 10:00 PM**).
- Remove laundry promptly; do not leave clothing unattended for long periods.
- Clean lint traps after each use; report broken machines to the managing agent.

## 9. Occupancy, Guests & Subletting
- Apartments are for residential use only by the shareholder and the persons
  named in the proprietary lease/stock certificate.
- Short-term rentals (Airbnb, VRBO, and similar) are **prohibited**.
- Subletting requires Board approval and a completed **Sublet Application**;
  subtenants are bound by these House Rules.
- Guests staying longer than [confirm — e.g., 30 days] must be disclosed to the
  Board.

## 10. Safety & Security
- Do not prop open or disable building entrance, lobby, or stairwell doors.
- Do not admit ("buzz in") people you do not know.
- Report broken locks, lights, or security concerns to the managing agent
  promptly. For fire, gas, flooding, or other emergencies, call the building
  emergency line and 911 immediately.
- Smoke and carbon monoxide detectors must be kept operational; do not disable
  them.

## 11. Windows, Terraces & Building Exterior
- Window guards are required where mandated by NYC/NYS law for units with
  children under 11 (confirm applicability for Yonkers).
- Nothing may be hung, shaken, or dried from windows, railings, or fire escapes.
- No signs, awnings, antennas, satellite dishes, air conditioners, or other
  fixtures on the exterior or in windows without Board approval. Window A/C units
  must be installed safely and per any building standard.

## 12. Smoking
- State the building's policy here (e.g., smoking prohibited in all common areas;
  confirm whether in-unit smoking is restricted). Cannabis follows the same
  policy as tobacco.

## 13. Plumbing & Maintenance Responsibility
- Do not put grease, wipes, diapers, or non-flushable items down drains/toilets.
- Report leaks immediately to limit damage. Shareholders are responsible for
  damage caused by their own fixtures, appliances, or neglect.
- Provide reasonable access for building staff to make repairs or inspections
  with appropriate notice (immediate access in emergencies).

## 14. Insurance (HO-6) — Required
- Every shareholder must maintain an in-force **HO-6 unit-owner / co-op
  policy** at all times. See the separate **Insurance Requirements** document for
  minimum coverage amounts and the additional-insured/certificate requirements.
- Subtenants must carry **HO-4 renter's coverage**.

## 15. Enforcement & Fines
- Violations may result in written notice, fines, and — for repeated or serious
  violations — action under the Proprietary Lease.
- Suggested fine schedule (confirm amounts): first notice — warning; second —
  **$[amount]**; subsequent — **$[amount]** per occurrence. Shareholders are
  responsible for violations by their household members, guests, and subtenants.
- Fines and unreimbursed costs may be added to maintenance as additional rent.
