Insurance Requirements

HO-6 coverage required of every 555 McLean Ave shareholder.

Every shareholder must carry an in-force HO-6 policy with a minimum of $300,000 in personal liability, naming the cooperative and managing agent as additional insured. Subtenants must carry HO-4 renter's coverage.

Who must be insured

  • Every shareholder must maintain a current HO-6 policy (the unit-owner / co-op apartment policy) for as long as they own shares.
  • Every subtenant must maintain an HO-4 renter's policy.
  • Proof of coverage is required at closing and at every annual renewal.

Minimum required coverage

Personal liability$300,000
Dwelling / betterments & improvements (Coverage A)Enough to fully repair the unit's interior, fixtures, flooring, and improvements
Loss of use / additional living expenseIncluded per standard HO-6
Loss assessment$50,000
Water damage / sewer & drain backupIncluded (endorsement required)
Deductible$1,000 or less

Required endorsements & naming

The policy and the Certificate of Insurance must:

  • Name the cooperative corporation as additional insured.
  • Name the managing agent as additional insured / certificate holder.
  • List the co-op and managing agent as certificate holders so they receive notice of cancellation or non-renewal.

Proof of insurance

  • Submit a Certificate of Insurance (COI) showing the above to the managing agent before closing and again at each renewal.
  • If coverage lapses, the Board may obtain force-placed coverage at the shareholder's expense; a lapse is a violation of the House Rules and Proprietary Lease.

Why HO-6 matters

The co-op's master policy covers the building structure and common areas — it does not cover the interior of your apartment, your belongings, your liability, or upgrades you made. An HO-6 policy fills that gap and protects you (and your neighbors) if, for example, a leak from your unit damages the apartment below.

Questions about these requirements? Contact the Board or the managing agent. For policy specifics, consult a licensed insurance broker.
