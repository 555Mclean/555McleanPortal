Move-In / Move-Out Policy

555 McLean Ave — a resident-owned cooperative in Yonkers, NY.

Every move must be scheduled in advance with the managing agent, take place Monday–Saturday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, and is covered by a refundable $500 deposit. No moves on Sundays or legal holidays.

1.Schedule in Advance

  • Notify the managing agent and reserve your move date at least five (5) business days in advance. Dates are assigned first-come, first-served and only one move is permitted per day.
  • No move may begin until it has been confirmed in writing and the deposit has been received.

2.Permitted Days & Hours

  • Moves are allowed Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
  • No moves on Sundays or legal holidays. All moving activity — including elevator use and loading — must be finished by 5:00 PM.

3.Move Deposit

  • A refundable deposit of $500 is required before the move and held to cover any damage to common areas (lobby, hallways, elevator, doors, floors).
  • The deposit is returned in full after a post-move inspection finds no damage. The cost of any repairs is deducted from the deposit; charges beyond the deposit are billed to the shareholder.

4.Elevator Reservation

  • The elevator must be reserved for your scheduled move and will be padded by building staff beforehand.
  • Do not block, prop, or disable the elevator. Be considerate of neighbors who share it; keep the lobby and hallways clear during loading.

5.Movers' Insurance

  • Professional movers must provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI) naming the cooperative and the managing agent as additional insured before the move.
  • The building is not responsible for damage to or loss of a resident's belongings during a move.

6.Protecting Common Areas

  • Protect floors, walls, and doorways. Do not drag furniture across lobby or hallway floors.
  • Remove all boxes, packing material, and debris the same day — do not leave them in hallways, stairwells, the trash area, or by the dumpster. Bulk discards must be arranged separately with the managing agent.
  • Do not block building entrances, driveways, fire lanes, or other residents' parking spaces with the moving vehicle.

7.New Shareholders — Before You Move In

  • Provide proof of an in-force HO-6 insurance policy meeting the co-op's Insurance Requirements (minimum $300,000 personal liability, co-op and managing agent named as additional insured).
  • Review and agree to the House Rules.
  • Confirm your contact and emergency information with the managing agent.

8.Unscheduled Moves & Penalties

  • Moving without a confirmed reservation, outside permitted hours, or without the required deposit may result in forfeiture of the deposit and a fine, and the move may be stopped.
  • Shareholders are responsible for the conduct of their movers and for any damage they cause.
Questions or to schedule a move? Contact the Board or the managing agent.
