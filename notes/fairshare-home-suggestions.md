# FairShare Home — iOS Suggestions & Change Ideas

## iOS-Specific Features Worth Adding

- **Widgets** — Home screen widget showing today's tasks and point balance. Key for daily engagement, especially for kids.
- **Siri Shortcuts** — "Hey Siri, mark the dishes done" or "Hey Siri, what are my tasks today?"
- **Live Activities** — Show a task countdown on the Lock Screen when a deadline is close.
- **Focus Mode Integration** — Tasks surface as notifications only during non-school/non-work hours for kids.
- **iCloud Sync** — Household data syncs across all family iPhones/iPads without extra setup.
- **Family Sharing** — Tie into Apple's existing Family Sharing group so adding household members is instant.

---

## UX / Design Considerations

- **Separate app experiences per role** — Members (especially kids) should see a simplified, visual UI. Admins see the full dashboard. Same app, different views based on role.
- **Onboarding flow** — Walk admins through creating their first household, adding members, and assigning a task in under 2 minutes.
- **Task completion animation** — Satisfying confetti or point-pop animation when a member marks a task done.
- **Streak tracking** — "Jayden is on a 5-day streak!" Builds habit and engagement.

---

## Feature Changes to Consider

| Current Idea | Suggested Change | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Admin approves every task | Make approval optional per task | Low-stakes tasks shouldn't need approval — reduces admin burden |
| Points only | Points + optional real dollar allowance tracking | Broader appeal for parents who pay actual allowances |
| One household per account | Multiple households in free tier (limited members) | Useful for divorced parents with two homes |
| Custom reward store (paid only) | Allow 3 custom rewards in free tier | Lowers the barrier to experiencing the best feature |

---

## Monetization Tweak

- Use a **family plan** pricing model instead of per-person:
  - Free: 1 admin, up to 3 members
  - Plus ($4.99/mo): unlimited members, premium features
- Familiar to iOS users already used to Apple One / family subscriptions.

---

## Things to Reconsider for v1

- **Google Calendar sync** — Apple users skew toward Apple Calendar. Save Google sync for v2.
- **Airbnb/property manager version** — Great long-term idea but a separate product. Keep out of v1 scope.
- **AI chore suggestions** — Strong differentiator but save for v2. Don't let it delay launch.

---

## Biggest Quick Wins

- **Widgets + Apple Family Sharing integration** — Native iOS, low effort to prototype, makes the app feel at home on iPhone immediately.
